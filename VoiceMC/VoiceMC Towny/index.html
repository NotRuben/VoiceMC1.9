Towny Rules

Network rules apply
1. Overloading areas with entities is forbidden (using/building laggy builds is forbidden).
1.1. This also counts for using the pshop to withdraw too many items.
2. Griefing towns is forbidden (this also includes town members).
2.1. Building around towns to stop them from playing normally is forbidden (except you are in the town).
2.2. If a town can not pay for daily upkeep and it falls these rules do not apply anymore.
3. Trolling (in any way) is forbidden.
4. Using traps is forbidden. Counting as traps are:
• Any redstone device that harms a player.
• Any town spawn that does not allow safe entry and/or exit.
• Large holes that are not properly secured.
• TP Traps.
5. War (between towns) is normally disallowed.
5.1. War can be allowed by an Administrator (or higher) under the agreement of
•both Town Mayors / Nation Kings.
6. Stream-Sniping / Meta-Gaming is forbidden.

Admins have final say in the interpretation of any rules listed or not listed in this document.
Punishments: currently under the discretion of the moderator/admin.
Punishments may vary depending on many factors.
In all cases that is up to staff.
If somebody may break a rule that is not listed here but should/is forbidden staff can decide by themselves to punish it or not.
Breaking any rule can result in a permanent ban from our network - not depending on donating or how much you spent for the server.
Donating to the server does not protect from bans and else.
Rules may change depending on staffs decision - its every players own task to keep up on our rules.
